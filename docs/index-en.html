Los Barriles Ice Cream is a family-owned business, where we focus on keeping the Mexican tradition of barrel-made ice cream alive. Our ice cream is 100% natural and handcrafted, Jalisco-style, making you feel like you're tasting a piece of Mexico. We offer a variety of over 100 flavors, ranging from classics like vanilla, lemon, chocolate, and strawberry to the most exotic ones such as soursop, mamey, dragon fruit, prickly pear, arrayan... We rotate these flavors every week to ensure you always have a diverse selection and new tastes to try!


We started in 2014, initially selling only ice cream, and over the years, we have gradually added other delicious Mexican cravings to our menu. Some of these include salchipulpos, esquites, milkshakes, mini pancakes, crepes, strawberries with cream, biónicos, and many other snacks!


At Los Barriles Ice Cream, we take pride in our abundance and strive to serve your order generously, always using the finest ingredients. Thanks to this commitment, we have been warmly embraced by our customers in San Jose and throughout the entire Bay Area!


We also provide catering services for various events, including children's parties, quinceañeras, weddings, festivals, corporate events, and more.


So, if you haven't visited us yet, we invite you to check out our food truck, and soon, our physical location. You're going to love it!
